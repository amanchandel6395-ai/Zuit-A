Deploy — make the QR scannable (free, ~5 min)

1

Download the AR experience HTML file above.

2

Create a free account at github.com, then a new repository.

3

Upload the HTML file, then go to Settings → Pages and enable it for the main branch.

4

You'll get a live link like yourname.github.io/repo/file.html — paste it into any free QR generator (e.g. qr.io) to get a working scannable QR code.
